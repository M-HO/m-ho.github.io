Our Studio

Welcome to Studio M, located in the heart of Zürich, where creativity meets technical excellence. Our space is designed to provide a comfortable and inspiring environment for your audio projects.

Our studio is equipped with high-quality equipment and acoustically treated spaces to ensure the best possible sound for your productions. Whether you're looking for mastering, mixing, or production coaching, we have the tools and expertise to help you achieve your sonic goals.

We believe in a collaborative approach, working closely with artists to understand their vision and bring it to life. Our 'no frills, only skills' philosophy means we focus on what truly matters: delivering exceptional audio quality that makes your music stand out.

Our Team

Michal Holy

Mastering, Mixing, Coaching

Michal Holy has been producing his own and the music of other artists since the mid-nineties for labels such as Get Physical and Souljazz, in genres such as minimal techno/house, jungle/drum & bass, dubstep and avant-garde rock. He has been mixing and mastering for the Swiss label Waswotsch Music since 2020 and worked with numerous international artists. He has coded and released his own Max4live plugins, and worked as a developer for the music software industry.
